### :woman_technologist: &nbsp;About Me :

Hi! 
My name is Alexey. I'm an experienced Frontend developer with a passion for creating interactive and user-friendly web applications. With several years of experience in web development, I strive to turn complex design concepts into flawless interactive websites.

My strongest skill-set includes the following development areas: JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Redux, Redux-Saga, Redux-Toolkit, Thunk, SCSS, Storybook, FSD, Webpack, Cypress, Jest, REST, MongoDB and know how to work in Agile, SCRUM, Kanban, BEM . Collaboration is important to me and I enjoy working with cross-functional teams to bring innovative ideas to life. I am constantly exploring new technologies and best practices to be at the forefront of the ever-changing web landscape.

If you're looking for a dedicated and attentive Frontend developer who can turn your idea into a reality, don't hesitate to contact me. Let's create something amazing together!
